CREATIVE PROCESS


The Creation of Mad House


I was inspired to create a game based on my own personal experiences about
a moment of my life in which I felt like it was so bad-it couldn't possibly be real.
I wanted to tell a story to inspire others that the death of something can be a
chance for hope, peace, and a new beginning. And that the goodness in life comes from
a strong will, hard work, and determination.

I was inspired by Mortis Ghost's OFF, and Persona 3.



I also took inspiration from music, pinpointing lyrics and feelings that represented
a loss of hope, and a rediscovery of the self.
